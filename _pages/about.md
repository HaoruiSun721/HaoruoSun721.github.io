Haorui Sun is currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Mechanical Engineering at [Zhejiang University](http://me.zju.edu.cn/). He previously completed his undergraduate studies in Mechanical Manufacturing and Automation at the School of Mechanical Engineering, [Sichuan University](https://msec.scu.edu.cn/). His main research interests include Digital Aircraft Assembly and Intelligent Manufacturing.
